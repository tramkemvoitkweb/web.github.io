THE SILENT CITY

DEFINE YOUR OWN FUTURE

"The Silent City" is an action-adventure game that takes players on an extremely gripping journey alongside the former detective L.A.P.D Lukas Krausen. Lukas finds himself in the quest for finding justice in the city of San Roguina, USA, after being investigated for a prolonged period in connection with murder allegations related to a case he once worked on.

captivating gameplay

"The Silent City" offers players a perfect blend of intense action sequences and mind-bending puzzles, challenging your skills and intellect. Stealth, strategy, and investigation are your tools as you traverse the dark corners of the city.

stunning graphics

Immerse yourself in the stunning world of San Roguina, brought to life with meticulous detail and accompanied by a haunting soundtrack that heightens the suspense.

Unforgettable story

Lukas Krausen's quest for justice and freedom in the enigmatic city of San K. Roguina promises players an unforgettable gaming experience filled with suspense, mystery, and redemption. As players uncover the secrets of "The Silent City," they will find themselves drawn into a world where every dialogue, decision, choice and discovery shapes the destiny of offier Lukas Krausen and the city itself.

"The silent city" await your exploration.
