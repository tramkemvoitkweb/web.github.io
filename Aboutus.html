about the silent city

"The Silent City" is an action-adventure game that takes players on a gripping journey alongside former L.A.P.D detective Lukas Krausen. Lukas finds himself in the quest for justice in the city of San Roguina, USA, after being investigated for a prolonged period in connection with murder allegations related to a case he once worked on.

In "The Silent City," players assume the role of Lukas Krausen, a skilled and talented former investigator determined to clear his name. Faced with serious murder accusations linked to a past case, Lukas decides to return to the city of San Roguina to seek justice and prove his innocence.
